CYBROX

PROBLEM

Many people struggle to find reliable and personalized skincare advice often finding themselves on unverified online sources or spending loads of money on dermatological appointments. This lack of access to expert's guidance at ease can lead to misdiagnosis, ineffective treatments, and even more severe causes.

OUR SOLUTION

A user-friendly mobile app that offers personalized dermatology consultations through an AI-powered chatbot, providing detailed explanations about the issues and offering home remedies for common skin issues, along with virtual access to certified dermatologists for severe cases.
